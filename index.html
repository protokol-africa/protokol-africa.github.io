The netting thesis
On any bilateral corridor with two-way traffic, 80-90% of gross volume nets out. You only need to convert the residual. Applied across all African corridors simultaneously, this reduces settlement costs by an order of magnitude.

FAQ

Common questions.

Is this a cryptocurrency?
No. Protokol uses blockchain (Stellar) as a neutral settlement ledger between operators — not as a currency. End users never see crypto. Every unit is backed 1:1 by real fiat in regulated bank accounts.
Do operators need to change their systems?
No. One API connection. Operators keep their apps, agents, customers, brand. Protokol handles only the invisible settlement layer between them.
Who pays and how much?
Operators pay 0.05-0.10% per transaction — 5 to 20x less than current solutions. Pay-per-use, no minimum, no setup fee.
Is this competing with PAPSS?
No. PAPSS connects banks via central bank infrastructure. Protokol connects fintechs and Mobile Money operators via an open protocol. Complementary layers.
